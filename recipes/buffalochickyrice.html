Buffalo Chicken and Rice

A tasty combination of soft white rice, chicken, and hand picked avocado - topped with the best duo of them all. . . buffalo and ranch!

  • chicken breast
  • white rice
  • avocado
  • buffalo
  • ranch
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. cook rice in rice cooker
  2. cut chicken
  3. cook chicken on stovetop
  4. add chicken and rice to serving bowl
  5. add avocado
  6. add buffalo and ranch
