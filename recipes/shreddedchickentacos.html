Shredded Chicken Tacos

Tacos... juicy slow cooker shredded chicken tacos

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 Tbsp taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast

Steps

  1. combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bow
  2. place chicken in a slow cooker and pour chicken broth mixture over chicken
  3. cook on low for 6 to 8 hours
  4. shred chicken and make dem tacos
